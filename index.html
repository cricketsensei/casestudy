Case Study Between Google Lens And MobileNet

GOOGLE LENS VS MOBILENET

  1. Test Image -
    2. Output on GOOGLE LENS - Gaming Mouse
    Output on MobileNet Model - Vacuum Cleaner, vacuum

    Result -

    Google Lens is more accurate.


I tested 7 things. Those things were a mouse, a coin, a sharpener, water bottle, a marker, a LEGO, and a piggy bank. Out of the 7, Google Lens was able to find all 7 items. But MobileNet was able to find 4 out the 7 items. So this shows that Google Lens is more accurate.
